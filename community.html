About CHILL

CHILL is a local community group in Carrickfergus.
OUR AIM is to improve and promote positive mental health and wellbeing, to raise awareness of suicide prevention in the Mid & East Antrim Borough Council areas.

We care for our communuity

CHILL was formed in 2019, after a public meeting highlighted a lack of support for the local community in relation to mental health and suicide prevention We are a local grassroots community organisation and we have a real passion for helping people in anyway we can. We currently provide 1:1 counselling supporting children, young people and adults, peer support groups, training, awareness and activities in promoting positive mental health and suicide prevention.
