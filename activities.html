About CHILL

CHILL is a local community group in Carrickfergus.
OUR AIM is to improve and promote positive mental health and wellbeing, to raise awareness of suicide prevention in the Mid & East Antrim Borough Council areas.

Our Weekly Walk

At CHILL we have the community involved in our weekly Wednesday walks and chats
• We have two walks on the same night
• A short walk 30 minutes and a longer walk 60 minutes
• Walking helps our physical and mental health
Mental health
• releases feel good hormones which improves your mood, reduce stress and depression, manage anxiety, improves sleep, Increases energy, improves confidence and self esteem
Physical Health Benefits

Young Chillerz

The concept of “young Chillers” was grown from the experience of a 13 year old boy who suffered anxiety and struggled to attend school. Through his own experiences he found he wanted to help others.
• He was able to identify other class mates who were struggling, becoming overwhelmed, anxious, fidgeting or having behavioural changes.
• This lead to CHILL talking in schools in the Carrickfergus area and engaging with the youth in places they were congregating at the weekends
• The young Chillers have undertaken age appropriate training to enable them to signpost those in need of assistance
